Nonfiction Newspaper Article · Genre: Literary Journalism

A fictional newspaper interview formatted as authentic journalism, featuring DEA Agent Hank Schrader from Breaking Bad reflecting on the Heisenberg investigation. The assignment challenged me to perform within a real journalistic genre while working with entirely fictional subject matter.

Magazine Spread: Built From The Ground Up

Sports Journalism Layout · Genre: Magazine Feature

A full journalism-style magazine spread covering the Seattle Seahawks' Super Bowl LX championship run.
